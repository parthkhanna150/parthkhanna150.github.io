My favorite meal

Yes, it will make you lose fat, gain muscle :)

  • Chop potatoes into an inch or two cubes
  • Bring them to a boil
  • Meanwhile, caramelize the onion, can add some cherry tomatoes/jalapenoes
  • Then stir in your minced meat with the seasoning - 7minutes
  • Drain the water, spread the potatoes in a baking dish
  • Bake them for about 10minutes at 420 degrees Fahrenheit
  • Take out the dish, add your minced meat and onion mix, top it with cheese and broil on high for another 3-4 minutes.
